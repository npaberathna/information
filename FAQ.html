FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Your queries answered. Find everything you need to know about shopping with Food City!

1. How do I place an order online?

To place an order, browse through our products, add items to your cart, and click on the checkout button. You will be asked to provide your delivery information and payment method to complete your purchase.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments like eZ Cash and mCash. You can also pay upon delivery using cash.

3. Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer home delivery to most locations. Delivery charges may vary depending on your location and the total value of your order.

4. Can I modify my order after placing it?

Once an order is placed, you cannot modify it directly. However, you can contact our customer service within 30 minutes of placing the order to request changes, if possible.

5. How can I track my order?

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order status in the "My Orders" section of your account.

6. What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with a product, you can return it within 7 days of delivery, provided it is in its original condition. Perishable items like fresh produce cannot be returned.

7. What if an item is out of stock?

If an item is out of stock, we will notify you and you can choose whether to cancel the order or replace the item with an alternative. We do our best to maintain accurate stock levels on the website.

8. Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it. Once the order is processed for delivery, cancellation may not be possible. Please contact customer service for assistance.

9. How long does delivery take?

Delivery usually takes 1-2 business days, depending on your location. In some cases, delivery may take longer during peak times or due to unforeseen circumstances.

10. Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, there is a minimum order value of Rs. 500 for delivery. For pickup orders, there is no minimum value.
